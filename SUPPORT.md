# SurfSense Support

## How to Get Help

1. **Search existing issues** — Check the [issues list](https://github.com/Aimino-Tech/surfsense-issues/issues) to see if your problem has already been reported or resolved
2. **File a new issue** — If your issue is not covered, [create a new issue](https://github.com/Aimino-Tech/surfsense-issues/issues/new/choose) using the appropriate template
3. **Check documentation** — Visit the [main SurfSense repository](https://github.com/Aimino-Tech/SurfSense) for documentation and guides

## SLA Tiers

| Tier | Users | Response Time | Resolution Time | Channel |
|------|-------|---------------|-----------------|---------|
| Free | All users | 48 hours | 14 days | GitHub issues only |
| Premium (Writing Agent) | Paid tier | 4 hours | 48 hours | GitHub + Linear tracking |
| Enterprise | $500/month | 1 hour | 8 hours | GitHub + Linear + Priority |

### Definitions

- **Response Time**: Time until a human or AI first responds to your issue
- **Resolution Time**: Time until a fix or workaround is provided
- **Response Time SLA** applies during business hours (Monday-Friday, 9AM-5PM UTC)
- **Critical severity** issues are escalated outside business hours

### Premium Tier (Writing Agent)

Users with an active Writing Agent subscription receive:
- Prioritized triage within 4 hours
- Dedicated tracking in Linear for visibility
- 48-hour resolution target

### Enterprise Tier

Enterprise customers ($500/month) receive:
- Priority response within 1 hour
- Escalation to senior engineering team
- 8-hour resolution target
- Direct communication channel

## What to Include in Your Report

To help us resolve your issue quickly, please include:

- **SurfSense version**: Run `git log -1 --oneline` in your SurfSense directory
- **Deployment method**: Docker Compose, Desktop App, Browser Extension, or Obsidian Plugin
- **Steps to reproduce**: Exact steps that lead to the issue
- **Expected vs actual behavior**: What you expected vs what happened
- **Screenshots or logs**: If applicable

## Issue Response Flow

1. Issue is filed via the appropriate template
2. AI triage classifies the issue, applies labels, and checks for duplicates
3. Team reviews and prioritizes based on severity and support tier
4. Issue is resolved and closed with a reference to the fix

## Escalation

If your issue requires urgent attention:
- **Critical severity**: Automatically escalated to on-call engineer
- **Premium/Enterprise**: Priority SLA applies
- **No response within SLA**: Bump the issue thread with a comment

## Feedback

We continuously monitor issue resolution quality. If you're unsatisfied with our response, please comment on your issue with "Feedback: [your concern]" and a team member will review.
